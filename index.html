Demo of Podify after Spotify Premium login and whitelist

Unlock Transcription Features

  1. Download the Podify Magic Transcriber from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Enter your email linked to your Spotify Premium account to join the whitelist features won't be active until then
  3. Add the extension to Chrome.
  4. Click on the extension icon in the Chrome toolbar when on the Podify tab.
  5. Click "Start Recording" in the new popup window to begin transcription.
